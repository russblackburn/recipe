Recipes

When you’re making a recipe, click on each direction as you complete it. The color will fade to show that you have completed that step, and the ingredient will be subtracted from the list of ingredients. Let’s say a recipe calls for 3 cups of milk, and the first step is to use 2 cups of milk. Once you click on that step, saying that you did it, the ingredients will say 1 cup of milk instead of the 3 cups of milk. I love this feature because it helps to prevent me from jumbling up a whole recipe and wasting ingredients simply from misreading a direction.

Nutritional Info

For every recipe, you will find a section filled with its nutritional information. This can help you determine which recipes are right for your own dietary needs. I have included calories as well as the major essential nutrients and those of typical interest.

I hope this website can help you reach your nutritional needs and goals. Enjoy!
